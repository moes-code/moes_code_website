App Privacy Policy

General information

This Privacy Policy describes how Moe's Code ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in our mobile applications available on Google Play Store.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or information from our users.

Our applications are designed with privacy in mind:

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No personal information is collected or stored on our servers
  • No user data is transmitted to third parties
  • No tracking or analytics services are implemented
  • No advertising networks are integrated

Data Storage

Any data generated or used by our applications (such as user preferences, app settings, or user-created content) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Remains under your complete control
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the application
  • Is not backed up to our servers

Open Source Nature

Our applications are open source, which means:

  • The source code is publicly available for inspection
  • You can verify that no data collection occurs
  • The community can audit our privacy practices
  • Complete transparency in how the app functions

Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with any third-party services that could collect your data, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, our applications are safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Since we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.
